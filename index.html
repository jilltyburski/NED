What is NED?

National Energy Distributors, Inc. (NED) provides a monitoring device that can reduce your fuel cost. The savings can be substantial.

A "NED Trak Gauge" monitors fuel consumption and fuel level.

The price of heating oil and propane can vary widely from dealer to dealer and county to county. NED continually monitors the fuel market and provides current prices and trends in real time to its customers. When our customers need fuel, we will shop to get the best price in your area at the time of purchase.

For Oil Delivery Companies

All homes with a NED Trak Gauge installed will have their fuel consumption rate and percent fuel remaining in a tank tracked. Seeing this information, oil delivery companies will be able to deliver only when necesssary.

This will allow the oil delivery companies to maximize the gallons delivered and reduce the number of deliveries per year resulting in substantial savings for them.
